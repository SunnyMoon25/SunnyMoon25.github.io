About this website:

Welcome to my Nick's Reviews. This website will be about all the movies, and tv shows that I have watched and what I think about them When I write my reviews I will give my honest feedback about them and what rating I think they deserve. I will also put the rating that popular websites like Rotten Tomato, IBDM, and other trusted sources give the movies and tv-shows. If you want to recommend a movie or tv show that you want me to watch and give a review about just go to the google form and fill it out.
